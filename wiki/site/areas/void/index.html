    Void

    The Farm is one of the first areas most Users go to. The farm contains only a few crops and farm animals, along with Billy and his sister Mandy who look over it!

    The Farm springs off into the Valley.

    QUESTS

    Thrall Thrashing from Khazix Summary:
    1. Vanquish 30 Void Thralls
    2. Bring 16 Thrall Eggs to Khazix
    Rewards:
    • 4x Thrall Balls
    • 300 EXP
    • 300 Slayer EXP
    • 300 Void Reputation
    • 250 Coins
    Worshipper Wonder from Khazix Summary:
    1. Harvest 50 Void Worshippers
    2. Bring 50 Void DNA back to Khazix
    Rewards:
    • 500 EXP
    • 500 Slayer EXP
    • 500 Void Reputation
    • 450 Coins
    Unblighted from Khazix Summary:
    1. Demolish 20 Void Sources
    Rewards:
    • Unblinder
    • 400 EXP
    • 400 Slayer EXP
    • 400 Void Reputation
    • 350 Coins
    Bulblin & Bullbo from Khazix Summary:
    1. Defeat both Bulblin & Bullbo
    Rewards:
    • Void Bulb Mount
    • 1,000 EXP
    • 1,000 Slayer EXP
    • 1,000 Void Reputation
    • 750 Coins
    Spiral Crystals from Khazix Summary:
    1. Destroy 12 Ender Crystals atop the Bedrock & Obsidian spires
    Rewards:
    • 500 EXP
    • 500 Slayer EXP
    • 500 Void Reputation
    • 450 Coins
    Joining the Chorus from Khazix Summary:
    1. Harvest 128 Chorus Flowers
    2. Deliver 128 Chorus Seeds to Khazix
    Rewards:
    • 2x Chorus Leaps
    • 500 EXP
    • 500 Slayer EXP
    • 500 Void Reputation
    • 450 Coins
