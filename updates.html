Update 2 (11/25/2020)

  • Daily Tokens Every 24 hours you can login to receive 1x Daily Token, which are a virtual currency, not physical items Talk to (New NPC) to trade the Tokens for some new rewards
  •  Engrams & Cryptarch Mobs around the world have a chance of dropping an Engram (Rare of Legendary) Take these Engrams to the Cryptarch in Town, and he'll decrypt them for you, rewarding you with a completely random item with a rarity of the same as the Engram I've created 20 entirely new items for the Engram, including: Staffs, Whips, Axes, Tools, Armor, & more Tasks will be added to the Cryptarch eventually 
  •  Hell Achievements Added some new achievements for slaying Hell creatures, Void Withers, & Ghastlys You likely cannot notice, but I've redone the back-end system for how these work compared to the Undead Achievements. They're very similar, but should be improved much more, and eventually I'll redo Undead the same way if this method proves to be better
  •  Battle Tags This is a new way to show you're achievements to other players Upon completing achievements (might expand this to other things) you unlock the ability to use a new Battle Tag Battle Tags appear next to your name in chat
  •  Changed each Level Up message, including Professions. No longer displays all Knowledge learned upon Level Up, refers to the the Menu to view them Added cool effect for each Level up 
  •  Voting You can now vote on 4 different sites to be rewarded! Checkout the sites here: https://www.adventurescraft.net/wiki/site/vote/ Voting can be rewarded every 24 hours
  •  Travels I redid the traveling system, although you won't be able to tell much. You can now use /warp AREA to quickly travel to any of the areas you already have unlocked.
  •  /Discord Added the command so you can get an invite while in-game!
  •  Home Guide Jenny, in the Town Hall has written a guide for you "Home Owners for Dummies!!" Check it out to learn everything you need!


Update 1 (8/16/2020)

  • Reaper Quest now completes for all players within 25 blocks of the Reaper
  •  Goblin Quest now completes for all players within 25 blocks of the Goblin 
  •  Wither Quest now completes for all players within 25 blocks of the Wither
  •  Magma Quest now completes for all players within 25 blocks of the Magma
  •  Dracula Quest now completes for all players within 25 blocks of the Dracula 
  •  Wither Mini-Boss Hitbox has been massively improved
  •  Castle Quest will now give you a point towards the total quests
  •  Castle Quest will now track the amount of Mordens you summon
  •  Enchantments should no longer be hidden
  •  Temporarily removed the Looting & Fortune Enchantments as they currently don't function
  •  Morden should no longer despawn
  •  Adjusted Bank Slots from 9 > 27
  •  Menu Item has been blacklisted from the Bank
  •  Farming EXP from Crops has been fixed
  •  Wheat, Carrots, Potatoes, Mushrooms, Mushroom Blocks, & Netherrack now have a 0.0002 (1/5000) chance to drop a Borg Box
    (The intent is to keep Borg Boxes rare, and these blocks allow you to farm them insanely quick, so they have a much lower chance to reward the boxes)
  •  Morden now wears Golden Armor rather than Iron, because he's not a noob
  •  Morden now drops his Flesh 100% of the time
  •  Morden, Void Magma, Wither, & Ghastly now properly drop their droptables
  •  Undead Damage has been redone, this will allow for a better Specific Damage system in the future
    TLDR: Mobs have Factions, this usually corresponds with their name such as Undead or Void (Name isn't always required for them to be in the Faction) If you have +50% Undead Damage, you now will get +50% Undead Damage again Undead Faction mobs, rather than just Zombies & Skeletons
  •  Added Knowledge: GUI access through the main menu which allows you to browse all Profession shops and craft things from anywhere
  •  Added Town Hall to the Map
  •  Created a "Skeleton Set" with some unique stats, they are rarely (1/1000) dropped from the Skeletons in the Courtyard
